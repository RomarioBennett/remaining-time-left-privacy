1. Overview

Remaining: Time Left ("Remaining," "the app," "we," "us," or "our") is a wellness reflection and time-awareness application. It allows a user to answer lifestyle and optional health-context questions, generates a longevity projection from bundled public-reference information and a local estimation model, and presents time-awareness views such as a countdown, life calendar, factor insights, and projection comparisons.

Remaining is designed to be private by design:

  • No account is required.
  • Assessment processing occurs on the user's device.
  • The current version does not send assessment answers or projection data to our servers because the app does not operate a backend service for those features.
  • The current version does not include advertising, analytics tracking, or behavioral profiling SDKs.
  • The user may delete locally saved projection data from within the app.
  • The user may choose to export a copy of projection data through Android's system share feature.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, why it handles it, where it is stored, whether it leaves the device, and the controls available to users.

2. Important Wellness and Medical Notice

Remaining is intended for general wellness reflection, education, and time awareness. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, prevention, risk certification, or a guaranteed prediction of lifespan or date of death.

The estimates shown by the app are projections based on population-reference data and user-provided inputs. Individual outcomes may differ significantly. Users should consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding medical conditions, symptoms, test results, treatment choices, or health-related decisions.

This policy applies to information handled through the Remaining Android application as presently implemented and distributed by the developer identified above.

This policy does not govern:

4.2 Information Calculated by the App

Based on your inputs, the app calculates information such as:

  • estimated life expectancy or projected age;
  • projected remaining time;
  • an estimated range;
  • baseline and current projection timestamps;
  • factor summaries showing influences that increased or decreased the projection;
  • life calendar and time-progress visualizations; and
  • working-time estimates based on app assumptions.

4.3 Information Stored on Your Device

The current version stores a local projection record in Android app storage using SharedPreferences. The saved record may contain:

  • baseline target timestamp and current projection target timestamp;
  • date/time values for when baseline and current projections were created or updated;
  • age at baseline assessment and age at current assessment;
  • baseline and current projected age;
  • estimated range;
  • selected country name and country code;
  • a local data/source note; and
  • a factor summary derived from the assessment results.

The current version also stores a local setting indicating whether Sensitive Mode is enabled.

The app processes questionnaire answers to calculate the result. It does not currently persist every raw question response as a complete assessment profile after calculation. However, the saved factor summary and projection record can reflect sensitive inferences about the inputs you provided.

The current version does not request access to:

  • contacts;
  • photographs, videos, or user media;
  • microphone or camera;
  • precise or approximate device location;
  • SMS messages, phone calls, or call logs;
  • device advertising identifiers;
  • Health Connect or wearable-device records;
  • payment or financial information; or
  • user accounts or login credentials.

If future versions add permissions, cloud services, analytics, Health Connect, wearables, subscriptions, accounts, or new data uses, this policy and relevant in-app/Play Console disclosures will be updated before those features are released where required.

5. How Information Is Used

Remaining uses the information handled through the app only to provide user-requested functionality, including:

  • calculating a local longevity projection and estimate range;
  • creating and maintaining the user's baseline countdown and current projection countdown;
  • displaying life calendar, progress, factor, insight, and improvement views;
  • supporting optional Sensitive Mode;
  • generating a user-requested export of the saved projection; and
  • allowing the user to delete locally stored projection data.

We do not use assessment information for:

  • targeted advertising;
  • selling data;
  • data brokerage;
  • marketing profiles;
  • insurance, credit, employment, or eligibility decisions; or
  • medical diagnosis or treatment.

6. Local Processing and Network Transfers

6.1 On-Device Processing

The app's estimate calculation and user-facing visualizations are performed locally on the Android device.

6.2 No Developer Backend Collection in the Current Version

The current app implementation does not include an app backend, an account system, analytics collection, advertising SDKs, or internet-based transmission of questionnaire answers or generated projections to the developer.

The application manifest currently does not request Android internet access permission.

6.3 Public Reference Data Is Bundled Locally

The app contains bundled local reference tables and source metadata derived from or informed by public sources, including:

  • World Bank World Development Indicators;
  • World Health Organization Global Health Observatory;
  • CDC / National Center for Health Statistics life expectancy references;
  • CDC PLACES health-risk-behavior reference material; and
  • CDC / NCHS NHANES reference material for advanced health-marker context.

Your assessment answers are not sent to the World Bank, WHO, CDC, NCHS, NHANES, or other source organizations by the app. Source names shown in the app identify the public information used to support its local estimation approach; they do not indicate that those organizations endorse the app.

7. Data Sharing, Sale, and Third Parties

7.1 No Sale of Personal or Sensitive Information

We do not sell your assessment information, health-related inputs, derived projections, or locally stored app data.

7.2 No Developer-Initiated Sharing of Assessment Data

In the current version, we do not transmit or disclose your locally processed assessment data or projection data to third parties through an app backend.

7.3 User-Directed Export

The app offers an export feature. If you choose Export My Data, the app prepares a projection export and opens Android's system share sheet. You decide whether to send or save that export and which external app or service receives it.

Once you choose to share exported information with another application, cloud drive, email provider, messaging service, or other recipient, that recipient's privacy practices govern its handling of the information. You should share projection data only with destinations you trust.

7.4 Software Components

The app uses Android/Jetpack user-interface libraries and the Lottie Android Compose library to render interface animations. As implemented in this version, these components are used for local app presentation and are not configured by the app to transmit assessment or projection information to the developer or advertising/analytics providers.

8. Storage, Security, and Protection Measures

The app stores saved projection information in Android private application storage. This storage is ordinarily isolated from other apps by Android's application sandbox, subject to the security of the user's device and operating system.

Privacy and security measures implemented in the current version include:

  • no user account requirement;
  • on-device calculation and local storage of projection results;
  • no backend transmission of user-entered health/lifestyle information in the current version;
  • no in-app advertising or analytics tracking SDKs in the current version;
  • user-accessible deletion controls;
  • user-controlled export through the Android share sheet; and
  • Android backup configuration intended to exclude locally saved projection data from cloud backup and device transfer restoration.

No storage or security method is guaranteed to be perfectly secure. Users should protect access to their devices using appropriate device security controls, especially if they consider their projection information sensitive.

9. Retention and Deletion

9.1 Retention

Saved projection information remains stored locally on your device until one of the following occurs:

  • you delete local app data from the app's Settings screen;
  • you clear the app's storage through Android system settings;
  • you uninstall the app, subject to Android/device behavior; or
  • the app is otherwise removed or its local storage is erased.

Because the current version does not transmit your assessment or projection record to a developer backend, we do not maintain a server-side copy for you to request from us or ask us to erase.

9.2 Delete Local Data in the App

You may delete saved projection data by going to the app's Settings area and choosing Delete Local Data. The app asks for confirmation before removing the locally saved projection record and returning to setup.

9.3 Export Before Deleting

If you want a copy of your saved projection before deletion, use Export My Data before deleting local data. Exports saved or shared outside the app are not automatically deleted when you delete data inside Remaining.

10. Backups and Device Transfers

Remaining is configured with Android backup settings intended to prevent the locally saved projection record from being backed up to Android cloud backup or restored during device transfer. This is intended to support the app's local-only privacy design and to prevent old projection records from unexpectedly reappearing after reinstallation or transfer.

Backup and device behavior may still depend on Android platform behavior, device manufacturer implementation, operating system version, or user-controlled external exports.

11. Children's Privacy and Intended Audience

Remaining's assessment flow is designed for adults and supports assessment ages of 18 to 100. The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a developer backend.

If you believe a child has used the app in a manner that raises a privacy concern, contact us using the privacy contact information at the top of this policy.

12. International Users

The app may be made available to users in multiple countries and offers selection of countries to support population-reference estimates. In the current version, user-entered assessment information and generated projection records remain locally processed and stored on the user's device unless the user elects to export them.

Users may have privacy rights under laws applicable in their jurisdiction. Because the current version does not maintain an account or server-side assessment database, the primary mechanisms to access or erase saved projection information are the on-device export and delete controls described above.
